[alchemical](alchemical.md)  [consumable](consumable.md)  [drug](drug-gmg.md)  [ingested](ingested.md)  [poison](rules/traits/poison.md)  

- **Price** 4 gp
- **Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** L
- **Activate** [Interact](interact.md)
