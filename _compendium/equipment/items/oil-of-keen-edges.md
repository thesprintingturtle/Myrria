# Oil of Keen Edges *Item 11*  
[consumable](consumable.md)  [magical](magical.md)  [oil](oil.md)  [transmutation](transmutation.md)  [uncommon](uncommon.md)

- **Usage** held in 2 hands; **Bulk** L
