[alchemical](alchemical.md)  [consumable](consumable.md)  [ingested](ingested.md)  [poison](rules/traits/poison.md)  [uncommon](uncommon.md)

- **Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** L
- **Activate** [Interact](interact.md)
