# Spike Snare *Item 1*  
[consumable](consumable.md)  [mechanical](mechanical.md)  [snare](snare.md)  [trap](trap.md)  

- **Price** 3 gp
- **Category** Snare

This basic snare consists of [hidden](conditions.md#Hidden) spikes that rely on a creature's momentum to lacerate or potentially impale it as it enters the snare's square, dealing `2d8` piercing damage. The creature must attempt a DC 17 basic Reflex saving throw.

*Source: Core Rulebook p. 591*
