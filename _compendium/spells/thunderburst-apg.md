# Thunderburst *Focus 3*   
[air](air.md)  [cursebound](cursebound-apg.md)  [evocation](evocation.md)  [oracle](rules/traits/oracle-apg.md)  [sonic](sonic.md)  [uncommon](uncommon.md)  

- **Mystery** Oracle
- **Cast** [>>](chapter-9-playing-the-game.md#Actions "Two-Action") 
- **Range**100 foot
- **Area**20-foot burst
- **Saving Throw** Fortitude

You create a powerful blast of air and a loud peal of thunder, dealing `2d6` bludgeoning damage and `2d6` sonic damage.

Each creature in the area must attempt a Fortitude save.

> [!success-degree] 
> - **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
> - **Success** The creature takes half damage.
> - **Failure** The creature takes full damage and is [deafened](conditions.md#Deafened) for 1 minute.
> - **Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage and is [deafened](conditions.md#Deafened) for 1 hour.

**Heightened (+ 2)** Increase each type of damage by `2d6` and the area by 5 feet.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 234*
