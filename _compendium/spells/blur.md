# Blur *Spell 2*   
[illusion](illusion.md)  [visual](visual.md)  

- **Traditions**: [arcane](arcane.md), [occult](occult.md)
- **Cast** [>>](chapter-9-playing-the-game.md#Actions "Two-Action") 
- **Range**touch
- **Targets**1 creature
- **Duration**1 minute

The target's form appears blurry. It becomes [concealed](conditions.md#Concealed). As the nature of this effect still leaves the target's location obvious, the target can't use this concealment to [Hide](rules/actions/hide.md) or [Sneak](sneak.md).

*Source: Core Rulebook p. 321*
