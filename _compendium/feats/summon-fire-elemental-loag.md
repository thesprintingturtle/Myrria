# Summon Fire Elemental  *Feat 13*  
[ifrit](ifrit-b2.md)  


You can summon an elemental ally. Once per day, you can cast [summon elemental](../spells/summon-elemental.md) as a 5th-level primal innate spell, but the elemental summoned must be a fire elemental.

*Source: Lost Omens: Ancestry Guide p. 104*
