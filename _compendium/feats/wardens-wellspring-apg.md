# Warden's Wellspring  *Feat 18*  
[ranger](rules/traits/ranger.md)  

- **Prerequisites**: warden's focus

You command unparalleled focus when you commune with the world around you. If you have spent at least 3 Focus Points since the last time you [Refocused](refocus.md), you recover 3 Focus Points when you [Refocus](refocus.md) instead of 1.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 133*
