# Fortuitous Shift  *Feat 9*  
[gnome](gnome.md)  

- **Prerequisites**: [Unexpected Shift](unexpected-shift-locg.md)

You have grown more comfortable with your penchant for supernatural disappearance. The flat check DC of your Fortuitous Shift feat decreases to 11, and you are no longer [dazzled](conditions.md#Dazzled) if you succeed.

*Source: Lost Omens: Character Guide p. 33*
