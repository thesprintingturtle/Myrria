# Expert Backstabber  *Feat 4*  
[archetype](archetype.md)  

- **Prerequisites**: [Assassin Dedication](assassin-dedication-apg.md)

When you [Strike](strike.md) a [flat-footed](conditions.md#Flat-footed) foe with a weapon that has the [backstabber](backstabber.md) weapon trait, you deal 2 extra precision damage instead of 1. If the weapon is a +3 weapon, you deal 4 extra precision damage instead of 2.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 158*
