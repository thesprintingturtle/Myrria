[archetype](archetype.md)  [auditory](auditory.md)  [transmutation](transmutation.md)  

- **Prerequisites**: [Nantambu Chime-Ringer Dedication](nantambu-chime-ringer-dedication-sot2.md)

- **Activity** Two-Action
